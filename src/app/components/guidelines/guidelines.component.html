How much to Invest

You do not need a large sum of money to invest in crypto assets but you should allocate no more than 2-5% of your investable funds into Crypto Assets. There are smart speculations, so don't bet the farm on them.

Use Dollar Cost Averaging (DCA)

Dollar Cost Averaging (DCA) is a simple investment technique that involves making regular fixed investments (as opposed to investing a large chunk at once) to accumulate wealth over time. By taking this approach you are mitigating risks and accumulating assets. For Example instead of investing 1 million you can invest 50,000 weekly or monthly until you reach your target returns.

Diversify

Invest in multiple crypto assets to mitigate risk and maximize potential profit. We advice you do your own research before investing in a crypto asset

Investment Holding period

In crypto asset investment patience is a virtue: Long term vision is the key for example if you invested four thousand Naira (4000) in Etheruem(ETH) in January 2017 by december 2017 your investment would be worth Four hundred thousand Naira (400,000), Etheruem rose over 5000% in 2017 the entire crypto asset market went from 18 billion dollars to 700 billion dollars. For best returns a few months to a couple of years can be a good idea.

Gains

Bigger market captitalision coins give you nice long term steady growth as the whole market matures but smaller market capitalization give you more gains but may also lose value that is why you need to diversify your Portfolio to mitigate risk.
